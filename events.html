Events

Little moments that become big memories.

Throughout the year we celebrate colors, stories, festivals, birthdays, sports days, and parent-child activities that make nursery life joyful.

These events help children build confidence and connection while enjoying the rhythm of school life.

ANNUAL EVENTS

Color Day

Children come dressed in a chosen color and take part in matching games, songs, and art work.

Story Day

Teachers bring stories to life with puppets, pictures, and simple role play activities.

Festival Fun

We celebrate festivals with crafts, music, and age-appropriate cultural learning.
