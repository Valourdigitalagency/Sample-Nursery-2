Academics

Learning through play, rhythm, and discovery.

Our nursery curriculum is built around the age and pace of each child. We combine songs, stories, games, drawing, movement, and sensory play to build early confidence.

Every day includes short focused sessions and joyful activities that support language, social skills, motor development, and early numeracy.

PROGRAM OVERVIEW

Nursery

  • Language building
  • Colors, shapes, and sounds
  • Music and movement

Lower Kindergarten

  • Letter and number readiness
  • Story time and group play
  • Fine motor practice

Upper Kindergarten

  • Early reading and writing
  • Simple math games
  • Classroom routines and independence

Teaching Style

  • Gentle teacher guidance
  • Activity-based learning
  • Warm encouragement every day
